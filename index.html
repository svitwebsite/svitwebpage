Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust in Global Import & Export

Founded in 2020, Sri Vaarahi International Trading Exports has grown from a small trading firm into one of the most trusted names in international commerce. We specialize in sourcing, processing, and delivering premium agricultural products, textiles, engineering goods and packaged foods to clients across 5+ countries.

Our team of 50+ dedicated professionals manages the entire supply chain with precision, transparency and commitment to excellence — from field to foreign market. With 12 product categories, we are your one-stop partner for all international trade requirements.
